Spaghetti Pomodoro

The family meal spaghetti pomodoro from "the bear"

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole white onion, halved
  • olive oil
  • half a stick of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • tomato paste
  • crushed red chili
  • Two 28 ounce cans, San Marzano tomatoes (about 1.6 kg)
  • 1 kg spaghetti
  • parmesan cheese
  • fresh basil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • italian herb seasoning (optional)

Steps:

  1. Coat pan in olive oil, melt half a stick of butter, salt the oil and fat, and place onion halves into oil for 4 minutes, medium-low heat.
  2. Add chopped garlic and stir.
  3. Add crushed red chili and tomato paste and stir for 2 minutes.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes into mixture and stir well.
  5. Once boiling, reduce heat to a simmer and cook for at least 45 minutes. Stir and taste often, season with salt until desired acidity is achieved. Add italian herb seasoning if desired.
  6. Add pasta to salted boiling water and cook until al dente, then add to sauce and mix.
  7. Top with grated parmesan cheese and fresh basil. Enjoy.
