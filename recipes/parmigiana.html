Chicken Parmigiana

Chicken Parmigiana, best paired with Spaghetti Pomodoro

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast fillet
  • Flour
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • 4 eggs
  • parmesan cheese
  • salt
  • pepper
  • italian herb seasoning (optional)
  • Fried chicken seasoning (optional)
  • Peanut or canola oil, if frying

Steps:

  1. Prepare breading station. Three plates or trays.
    1. Flour mixed with chicken seasoning and italian herb seasoning.
    2. 4 beaten eggs.
    3. Panko breadcrumbs
  2. Butterfly chicken breasts and beat with meat pounder or fists. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Coat with flour mixture, then eggs, then breadcrumbs.
  4. Into hot oil until crispy and golden brown. Or into oven or air fryer at 180 degrees celsius until browned, turning each side.
  5. If baked, top chicken with parmesan and return into oven at 150 degrees celsius until cheese melts on top.
  6. Serve with pasta. Enjoy.
